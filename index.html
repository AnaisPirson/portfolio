About

Myself

Hello. I'm Anais and I'm a Brit/Belgian from France but living in the Netherlands. After graduating from university in 2014, I found a job in the marketing industry as advertising operation specialist. This super techy role allowed me to discover the joys of HTML and JavaScript. So much so, I decided to complete a masters degree in Computer Science in 2018. Then Brexit happened so I packed my bags and headed off to the Netherlands to continue my career in AdOps. However a few year down the line and my love for coding is evermore present. I now want to take the final plundge and transition into a career where I can carry out my passion. Let's see what the future holds for me!
